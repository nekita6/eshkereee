All photographers want to see per image rates but most retouchers are reluctant to make quotes until they see the images. There's a reason for this: All jobs are wildly different. However, I can offer some "per image" rates as guidelines:

Head Shots/Portraits:
Primary focus on bringing out the most of a model's unique personality and style. Preserving and enhancing the subject's individuality while perfecting the overall image  20$

Editorial, Fashion:
Special attention paid to composition, color and high-end (no blur) skin retouching. Emphasis on clothing, accessories and building a cohesive style and story  30$

Glamour:
Emphasis on full body contouring, hair/makeup enhancement and fine detail skin work. More glossy than editorial fashion. Pure fantasy  45$

Beauty, Hair:
Pixel by pixel retouch including fine detail high-end skin retouching, contouring and hand painted enhancement of hair and makeup. Thorough perfection  65$

Extras:
Compositing, complex background swaps or other intense additonal tasks will be billed at  30$
