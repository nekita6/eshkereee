All photographers want to see their photos in perfect condition as quickly as possible, but most retouchers do not want to indicate specific prices until they see the images. Thus slowing down the workflow. There is a reason for this: all work is completely different. However, I can offer some “per image” rates as a guide:

Basic portrait retouching
Primary focus on bringing out the most of a model's unique personality and style. Preserving and enhancing the subject's individuality while perfecting the overall image. Elimination of minor skin defects — 7$

Editorial and Fashion
Special attention paid to composition, color and high-end (no blur) skin retouching. Emphasis on clothing, accessories and building a cohesive style and story — 15$

Glamour
Emphasis on full body contouring, hair/makeup enhancement and fine detail skin work. More glossy than editorial fashion. Pure fantasy — 25$

Beauty, Hair
Pixel by pixel retouch including fine detail high-end skin retouching, contouring and hand painted enhancement of hair and makeup. Thorough perfection — 35$
